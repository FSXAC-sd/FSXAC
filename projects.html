APSC Autonomous Claw Project

Objective: Using provided materials (aluminium, rubber bands, Arduino) to build an automatically triggering claw that can pick up various objects from cloth to soup cans.

We ultilized "Collaborative Sketching" to generate 6 different ideas, two of which was decided to be promising for us. Further developments was done on CAD software Autodesk Inventor and drawings were produced.

We used aluminium sheets and hand tools to manufacture claw parts using flat patterns from the CAD drawings. Experiments and risk analysis were made to make sure the claw performs to expectations.
